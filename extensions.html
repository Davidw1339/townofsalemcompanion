New Features!

Extensions are small features that expand the app's core functionality. Check back to see what new features we have planned. Feel free to suggest more extensions. To access extensions click on the small gear in the top navigation bar in the Ranked Practice page. Enable and disable extensions to your liking. Only one extension can be used at a time.

  • Question Generator

    To quickly generate questions, use the question generator extension to confirm the roles of townies. Enable the extension, and then click on the corresponding role to access a list of attributes and abilities as well as a randomly generated question that has a pre-defined answer. Check it out!

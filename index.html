Welcome!

Hello, this is a Town of Salem companion app to help you keep track of different roles during the game. This is mainly intended for players of Ranked and Ranked Practice.


Functionality:

Strikethrough!

Click on roles/categories to strike them out and eliminate options:



Filter Alignments!

If you don't know an alignment/category of role filter it out using the search box and let the app do the work for you:



Notes!

You are able to create, edit, and view notes for each specific role/category.
To create a note, right click on any role/category and type into the input box, then click OK.
To edit a note, right click on a role/category that a note has already been created for, then edit your note and click OK.
To view a note, hover over a role/category and you will be able to see what you have written.
